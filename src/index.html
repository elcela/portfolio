Projects

Bachelor's thesis: Optimization of AI algorithms to predict immunogenicity

This work explores computational methods to improve the prediction of immunogenicity in tumor-specific neoantigens for cancer immunotherapy. It uses the biological structure of neoantigens to produce features that predict each part of the immunogenic response in cells. By enhancing the previous state of the art models with advanced ML and deep learning techniques, the study achieves better predictive accuracy and identifies high-confidence neoantigens.

Aid Vision Pro

This project focuses on the design and development of AidVision Pro, a pair of smart glasses created to assist people with partial or total visual impairment in navigating their surroundings more safely and independently. The prototype combines hardware and software solutions to detect nearby obstacles and to recognize and classify people, cars, and other moving elements to deliver audio alerts to the user. The glasses are lightweight, ergonomic, and rechargeable, with customizable vibration and sound patterns, and include an emergency support system that allows caregivers to access video and provide real-time assistance.
